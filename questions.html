Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. Why are there only jobs from a few different cities?
A. Currently we are only posting jobs from cities in Southern California (Los Angeles, San Diego, Orange County). We hope to expand quickly, so if you love our app show your support by leaving a review and recommending us to your friends. The more support we get, the faster we can expand!
Q2. How do I apply for jobs from your app?
A. By clicking on a job, it will open the job details and you will see an apply button that will take you straight to the company website. However, we recommend using our email feature on the saved screen. While you are swiping through your feed, just save your favorite jobs and we will email you all the links so you can easily apply from your computer.
Q3. How do you recommend jobs?
A. Our algorithim uses a variety of data points from your profile (major, work experience, interests...) and also your activity on the app. Our goal is to provide relevant recommendations that fit your skillset AND interests.
Q4. Can I search for jobs directly?
A. This feature is currently unavailable, but if this becomes a popular request we will add it soon! Also, on our search screen you can apply different filters to return more specific results.
Q5. Does Higher have a website?
A. We are still working on developing a web application. This is something we are working on though, so you can expect it to be available in the future!
